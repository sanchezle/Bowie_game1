About Bowie Game

Welcome to BowieGame! Bowie is a wire-haired dachshund dog that was born on June 10, 2022, in Guadalajara, Madrid, Spain.

Bowie is one of the family dogs of the BowieGame creator. After spending a lot of time together and walking Bowie in Ciutadella Park, Barcelona, Spain, the idea for the game was born. Bowie's mother had left a couple of months before, taking Bowie to Margarita Island in Venezuela. This period of separation, coupled with the completion of the Harvard CS50x course, inspired the creation of BowieGame.
