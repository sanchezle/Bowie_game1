Instructions

  • The game lasts 60 seconds
  • Hit the pigeon and score 40 points
  • Pop as many bubbles as you can; each bubble popped adds 2 points to the score
  • Bowie barks when he reaches max height (Is Bowie actually barking)
  • On mobile, press the Jump button to jump and hold it to reach max height; on desktop, press the space bar
  • Move with the arrow buttons; on desktop, use the keyboard arrows instead
